The mission: Collect people

In a fleeting world, photographs are like tiny frames capturing life's essence. They freeze moments, preserving emotions and stories for eternity. Take some today.
We created a group for you to join and send pictures of yourself and the people you got to know today. We know it's hard to know whom to talk to and what about, so we created a list of characteristics you could search for when taking pictures.

  • somebody with a birthday in the same month as yours
  • somebody who is living at least 4000 km away from your hometown
  • somebody who knows at least three Bond actors
  • somebody who performs the same sport you do
  • somebody who does a really rare sport
  • somebody with the same height (+/-3cm)
  • somebody with the same shirt color
  • somebody in flip flops
  • somebody who ate a (extremely tasty) Quiche in the last week
  • somebody who lives in the same bâtiment as you do
  • somebody else
  • somebody who never drank a Martini
  • somebody with glasses (lunettes)
  • somebody who owns glasses (verres) in Gif
  • somebody whom you haven’t spoken to at all yet
  • somebody who is not in the same year as you are
  • somebody with a license to list sweater
